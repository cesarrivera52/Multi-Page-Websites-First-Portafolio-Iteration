Project Managment / NOM036 / Time and Movement

Audi México

February - June 2023

Situation

As it was mention before Audi México facility is in charge to produce globally Q5 models, the challenge it was presented to us was related with the modification of a deviced they used called Trolley to move component through the company.

This deviced represented a risk for the operators because in some practices they were moving to many weight overpassing the amount established in the NOM036.

Due to the complexity of the project it was needed to divide the group into different teams. We worked with a big multidisciplinary team since it was integrated with Mechanical & Industrial and Systems Engineers.
Each group of engineers worked in their specialized area, for example the mechanical engineers worked with the design of the device and the Industrial Engineers worked with the QFD, Ergonomic State, Time and Movements and corrective and Preventive Maintenance Plan.

There are shown 2 videos above, one is the mechanism we part as a reference or concept we wanted the prototype to be, based on the mechanism of the video shown in the left part of your screen. We wanted to replicate the concept where the user pulls back the object and it storages energy to be ejected fordwards to make easier to break the moment where the Trolley is not moving.
Imagine you are an operator whose job is to move parts from one area of the company to another. Over the long term, this job could cause chronic back damage and potentially leave you disabled for the rest of your life.

Results

The expected outcome of this project is a functional device that reduces the physical force operators need to perform their tasks. From an ergonomic perspective, the device complies with NOM-036 standards. Regarding our deliverables, my team and I developed a comprehensive, well-structured, and detailed manual.
