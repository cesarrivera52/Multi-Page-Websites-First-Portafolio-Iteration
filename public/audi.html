Situation

During my time as an Industrial ans Systems student I had the opportunity to work with the hand of our training partner Audi México.

Audi México located in San Jose Chiapa, Puebla, is a The Audi plant in San José Chiapa is dedicated to the production of high-end vehicles, such as the Audi Q5.
The process begins with the preparation of the body and its painting with premium finishes that meet international standards.
Next, internal components and engines are assembled, integrating electronic, mechanical, and safety systems.
Automated technology is combined with specialized human intervention to ensure precision and efficiency.
Strict protocols guarantee the consistency and quality of each vehicle produced.
We developed 2 different projects for different purposes. As the MR35 that is a device modified to increase the charge capacity and reducing the ergonomic danger it could represent for the operators at the time they are moving automotive parts from the warehouse to any part of the company.
The other project we developed was an AHP decision-making matrix to have an automatize matrix to help determine what components should leave the warehouse system.
