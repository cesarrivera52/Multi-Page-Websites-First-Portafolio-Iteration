Continuous Improvement

Process improvement system for IMPLAN Puebla

Lean Methodologies

April - July 2024

Situation

In this project we collaborate with the "Instituto Municipal de Planeacion" Puebla, this institute is in charge to

manage the security, economic development, building services, wellness for the community, and modern government. They rule their project to allign with the 2030 agenda.

It is a state entity that works in collaboration with multiple sub-entities that authorize, prioritize, and manage which projects will be carried out during the term of a government in office

To describe in a better way it was uploaded downwards a presentation of what we do at the first iteration.

Phase 1

The image bellow shows a Value Stream Mapping (VSM) representation of how the institute works. The VSM is a tool that allowed us to set how are the processes and where could it be bottlenecks.

In the case of the institute we identified that there was a processes with a big lead time to be accomplished and that is where we decided to put our effort to work on.

As you can see in the VSM there is a red box that shows the bottleneck, in this section the information is passed on to management to analyze the possibility of launching the project or if it is discarded.

We identified a big improvement area because they compiled the information really hard and not to efficient. They used a PowerPoint template and in most of the cases the data recollection took to many time.

Task

Once we had identified the multiple improvement areas, we started working with the advances.

We tought it was a great idea to condition an Excel Office Teams file were the operators had the process automatize, we also included new KPI´s added KPIs to have better control over determining the timing for carrying out the activities.
With this proposal value we had a forward step in the information quality, human error and digital communication between agencies.

We applied a simple solution for a complex problem with the objective of spending the less amount of money and time. It is important to mention we had 5 weeks to develop a functional proposal value project.

Action

The improvement between the current and future state lies in the automation of various functions that eliminate the need for IMPLAN staff to perform them manually.

This minimizes human errors and streamlines the filling of forms in a simple manner, allowing users to focus on variable elements depending on the entity, such as planned and completed activities, their objectives, and descriptions, which are independent of the rest and cannot be automated more effectively.

Results


As a result of what we presented of proposal value it will be shown downwards a pdf presentation of the results and the Excel File we developed.

We also created a dashboard that allows the user to visualize the data in a more interactive way, with the ability to filter by machine, date, and other parameters.
